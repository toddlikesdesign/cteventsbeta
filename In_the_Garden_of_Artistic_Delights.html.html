In the Garden of Artistic Delights

On Saturday, August 1, we produced a very special event to benefit the Regional Arts & Culture Council (RACC)’s grantmaking program for individual artists. This non profit fundraiser, called “In the Garden of Artistic Delights,” celebrated RACC’s 20th anniversary as well as the splendor of Portland's local artists with delicous food, tantalizing beverages, artistic discoveries sprinkled throughout the magical garden, a live auction and raffle.

This outdoor summer event took place Bella Madrona in Sherwood, also known as “The Gardens of Sampson and Beasley.” The evening featured a variety of  Artistic Discoveries, stationed throughout the garden that patrons could explore before dinner.  

These Artistic Discoveries, featured work from such local artists as Katie Shook, Dana Lynn Louis, David Eckard, Bobak, 
Risk of Change Garden Gnomes, Wobbly Dance, and Carla Rossi.

In addition to the live auction and raffles prizes, this fun, summery non profit fundraiser also featured performances and entertainment from Nat Hulskamp, Anjali Dance, en Taiko and Children of Chaos.

“In the Garden of Artistic Delights”, RACC's first-ever fundraising event for individual artsits, raised a fantastic sum of $36,000 and a full 100% of the proceeds will be awarded as grants to individual artists this fall and winter. These funds will help RACC support more extraordinary and innovative artists throughout the Portland metro area!

We had so much fun at this event! We are excited about the outcome and the future artists the RACC can support!

Thanks to all of the amazing sponsors, guests, volunteers, entertainers and featured artists. Please make sure to visit RACC's Facebook page for more photos and information about their amazing work.
